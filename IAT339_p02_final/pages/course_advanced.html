For Advanced Students

Are you interested in speaking like a native English speaker and learning more about North American culture? Our advanced lessons will help you speak confidently over a wide variety of topics, learn about Canada and enjoy North American entertainment.

You can either attend lessons individually or part of a course. All of our courses include a free 1-on-1 coaching session, ongoing support and a certificate for successful completion.

Confident Conversational English

Want to be able to confidently discuss your favourite topics in English with native speakers? Our Confident Conversationalist English lessons will help you speak with confidently about a wide range of subjects, ranging from Science and Technology to Celebrity Gossip. The lessons are for Late Intermediate and Advanced English learners.

Confident Conversationalist Course: $150 (10 lessons)

Individual Lesson: $20 (1.5 hours)

Business and Money

Do you follow business or want to more literate when it comes to money? Learn how to discuss about the stock market, investments and all other things related to money in English.

Cafe Conversations

Are you having trouble expressing yourself in English? Would you like to know how to use slang and correct your pronunciation? Have coffee with a native speaker and get feedback on your English to become a better speaker. Learn slang and colloquial expressions. For mid to late intermediate students.

Celebrity Gossip

Are you dying to talk with someone about the misadventures of famous people in English? In this lesson, you will talk about the latest in celebrity gossip with your instructor who will teach you terms that will help you chat about this with your friends.
