About

Mission

We aim to improve people’s lives through language, by helping them speak a new language fluently and confidently.

We do this by making language learning as safe, fun, interactive and real world as possible. We aren’t afraid to teach idioms, slang and vulgar expressions, the other 50% of language. We do this through friendly and caring instructors

Values

We make sure our lessons are a safe place for students to learn, ensuring our students are physically safe and feel safe making mistakes during all of our lessons. Our instructors are non-judgmental when they share their opinions. We make sure our students’ needs are met before our own. When we feel safe, we are free to learn.
